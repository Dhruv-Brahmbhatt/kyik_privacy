Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

SmartRide Assistant (“we”, “our”, “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information when you use our application to discover and book rides with Uber and other providers.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: Pickup and drop coordinates you enter in the app.
  • Basic Usage Data: When you request a fare estimate or click a booking link.

How We Use Information

We only use your information to:

  • Fetch fare estimates from Uber and other ride providers.
  • Generate deep links that redirect you to the provider’s app or website for booking.

We do not store, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Uber and other ride-hailing services through official APIs and deep links. When you click a booking link, you are redirected to the provider’s own platform, and their privacy policies apply.

Data Retention

We do not permanently store your trip details. All data is used in real time and discarded once estimates are displayed.

Your Consent

By using this app, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
